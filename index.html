The Authentic Pre-Order Difference

Many retailers keep tea stored on shelves for months, allowing it to lose its natural aroma, strength, and health benefits. We refuse to compromise. By bypassing traditional warehouses, we use a direct-from-factory sourcing model to guarantee the purest Sreemangal experience.

Strict Monthly Pre-Order Window

To guarantee absolute freshness, we operate exclusively on a monthly pre-order system. Orders are accepted only from mid-month to the end of the month. Once the ordering window closes, your tea is collected directly from the factory, packed fresh, and immediately dispatched.

Orders are strictly NOT accepted before or after this deadline.
